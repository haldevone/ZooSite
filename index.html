To Do

  • Kids Playground

    The Systementor Zoo playground is a vibrant, dinosaur‑themed adventure zone where children can climb, slide, and explore safely. Surrounded by towering replicas of gentle herbivores, the area features interactive fossil digs, jungle‑style swings, and shaded seating for families. It’s designed to spark imagination and let young explorers feel like they’re playing in a prehistoric world.

  • Restaurants

    At Systementor Zoo, dining is part of the adventure. Our restaurants are designed to let visitors enjoy delicious meals while overlooking habitats where real dinosaurs roam. From family‑friendly buffets with prehistoric‑inspired dishes to themed cafés serving quick bites, every venue offers a unique atmosphere. Guests can savor fresh food in safe viewing areas, combining comfort with the thrill of being surrounded by living giants of the Jurassic world.

  • Our great creatures

    Systementor Zoo is home to a breathtaking collection of real dinosaurs, carefully cared for in expansive habitats that recreate their prehistoric world. From towering herbivores like Brachiosaurus and Triceratops to formidable predators such as Tyrannosaurus rex and Velociraptor, each species offers visitors a rare glimpse into Earth’s ancient past. Guests can witness these magnificent creatures up close in safe viewing areas, experiencing the wonder and power of life from the Jurassic and Cretaceous eras.
